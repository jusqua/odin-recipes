Eggs Cake

⏱ 45 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup fluid milk
  • 100 grams butter
  • 1 soupspoon baking powder
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C);
  2. Mix eggs, butter, sugar and salt in a blender until smooth;
  3. Pour flour and milk and blend again;
  4. Add baking powder and blend in pulse mode alternately;
  5. Bake for about 30 minutes in the preheaten oven.
