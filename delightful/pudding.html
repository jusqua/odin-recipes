Pudding

⏱ 70 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 400 milliliter milk
  • 1 cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C);
  2. Mix eggs, condensed milk, milk in a blender until smooth;
  3. With the sugar, caramelize the cake pan;
  4. Bake for about one hour in the preheated oven.
