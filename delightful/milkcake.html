Milk Cake

⏱ 45 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 soupspoon butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 liter integral milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C);
  2. Mix eggs, butter and sugar in a blender until smooth;
  3. Mix cup flour and half milk;
  4. Mix the remainder until smooth;
  5. Grease cake pan and dump blender content;
  6. Bake for about 30 minutes in the preheated oven.
